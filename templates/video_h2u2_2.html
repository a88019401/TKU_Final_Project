Good morning, Yuki. You're an early bird.

Not me. My dog, Oreo, is the early bird. He usually wakes me up before six. Are you a morning person?

Yes. I wake up at five every day.

Good for you. I usually get up at seven. Sometimes, I get up after seven twenty.

Wow, that's late. What time do you usually go to bed?

At twelve.

Twelve? How come? What do you do after school?

I'm at cram school from six to nine. Then I go home and do my homework for two hours. I don't have much free time.

That's too bad.

I'm always tired in the morning. What can I do?

A shower can help. I often take a shower in the morning.

Really? I never do that. By the way, where are you going?

To the library.

Library? How often do you go there?

I check out books from the library three times a month.

What a bookworm! I go there about once or twice a year.
